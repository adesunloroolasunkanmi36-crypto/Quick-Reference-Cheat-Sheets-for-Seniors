üìû How to Make a Call

1 Tap the green Phone app on your home screen
2 Tap Keypad at the bottom of the screen
3 Enter the phone number using the number pad
4 Tap the green Call button
5 To end the call, tap the red End button
üí° Quick Tip: To call from your contacts, tap "Contacts" instead of "Keypad" and select the person's name.

üì∏ Send a Photo to Grandkids

1 Open the Photos app (colorful flower icon)
2 Find and tap the photo you want to send
3 Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
4 Tap Messages to text it or Mail to email it
5 Type the person's name or number, add a message, and tap Send
üí° Quick Tip: You can select multiple photos before tapping Share to send several at once!

üö® Use Emergency SOS

1 Press and hold the Side button and either Volume button together
2 Keep holding until the Emergency SOS slider appears
3 Drag the Emergency SOS slider to call emergency services
4 Your emergency contacts will be notified automatically
üí° Quick Tip: If you continue holding the buttons, your iPhone will automatically call emergency services after a countdown. Set up emergency contacts in Settings ‚Üí Health ‚Üí Medical ID.

üî§ Adjust Text Size

1 Open the Settings app (gray gear icon)
2 Scroll down and tap Display & Brightness
3 Tap Text Size
4 Drag the slider left (smaller) or right (larger)
5 The preview text shows you the size as you adjust
üí° Quick Tip: For even larger text, go to Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Display & Text Size ‚Üí Larger Text and enable "Larger Accessibility Sizes".

üé§ Quick Siri Commands

1 Say "Hey Siri" or press and hold the Side button
"Call [name]" - Makes a phone call
"Text [name] I'm on my way" - Sends a message
"What's the weather?" - Gets weather info
"Set a timer for 10 minutes" - Starts a countdown
"Remind me to take medicine at 3pm" - Creates reminder
"FaceTime [name]" - Starts a video call
"Read my messages" - Siri reads your texts
üí° Quick Tip: If "Hey Siri" isn't working, enable it in Settings ‚Üí Siri & Search ‚Üí Listen for "Hey Siri".
